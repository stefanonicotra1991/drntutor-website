About Me

A mathematician who loves to teach

My passion for mathematics, sparked in adolescence, has taken me across regions, countries, and continents, culminating in a PhD in Pure Mathematics from the University of Liverpool and a career as a secondary school maths teacher.

I believe that, with patience and the right guidance, anyone can master mathematics — not just for exams, but for life. Exams are milestones in a student's journey, fostering resilience and growth — not obstacles to fear.

Beyond mathematics, I'm a musician, singer-songwriter, husband, and recently a father. My interest in Buddhism, yoga, and meditation shapes how I approach teaching: with mindfulness, patience, and genuine care for each student's journey.
